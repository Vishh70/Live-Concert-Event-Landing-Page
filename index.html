Travel & Parking Guide

  • Metro + Cab: Use nearby metro stop, then ride-share to Gate A for fastest entry.
  • Parking: Paid parking is managed near Gate B and Gate C, subject to availability.
  • Drop-off Window: Recommended between 4:45 PM and 5:20 PM to avoid gate congestion.

Event Rules

FAQs

Is re-entry allowed after exiting the venue?

No, passes are valid for single entry only.

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, paid parking is available near Gate B and Gate C.

Can minors attend the event?

Guests under 18 must be accompanied by an adult and carry valid ID.

What time should I arrive for smoother entry?

Plan to arrive by 5:00 PM to complete screening and settle in before the first set.

Where can I get support inside the venue?

Visit the helpdesk near Gate A or approach the First Aid zone for urgent assistance.

What should I carry for faster check-in?

Carry a valid government ID, registration confirmation, and keep bags compact for quick screening.
